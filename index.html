Mapping Crime in Philadelphia

The city of Philadelphia provides data on Part 1 and Part 2 Crime Incidents through OpenDataPhilly.org. Part 1 & Part 2 Crime Incidents from the Police Department's INCT system with generalized UCR codes and addresses rounded to the hundred block. These counts may not coincide exactly with data that is submitted to the Uniformed Crime Reporting (UCR) system.

This application shows all of the crimes cateorized as Thefts or Fraud fromk 2006 to present, and breaks the data out in separate slides so that you can see only the events from 2016.

Each point on the map is interactive, so feel free to clik on a marker to get more specific information about the incident being mapped.
